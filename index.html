FAQ

Quick answers before we talk through the project.

How much does a WordPress website cost?

WordPress sites are $499 and include 3 months of ongoing support — maintenance, updates, and content changes. Final pricing may vary based on page count, e-commerce, integrations, and design complexity.

Do you offer ongoing website management?

Yes. Site management is $100 per month (first 3 months included with a new site) and covers updates, backups, analytics reporting, content edits, monitoring, and support.

Can you improve SEO and Google visibility?

Yes. SEO work can include technical cleanup, GA4, Google Search Console, page structure, metadata, performance improvements, and ongoing reporting.

Do you work with existing websites?

Yes. Existing WordPress and Drupal sites can be audited, cleaned up, updated, migrated, expanded, or managed month to month.

How do projects usually start?

We start with a short consultation, define the site goals, review any existing assets, then agree on scope, timeline, and the first deliverables.
